About Me

I am a multilingual scholar with a background in dance anthropology, mathematics, and music. I specialize in research related to dance education, flow psychology, and arts education.

Currently, I am completing my final semester of an Erasmus Mundus master's scholarship in dance anthropology and cultural heritage across four European universities.

Research & Publications

My research focuses on integrating arts education with interdisciplinary approaches to enhance creativity, academic growth, and mental well-being. I have worked on several projects exploring the relationship between dance, education, and cultural heritage.
